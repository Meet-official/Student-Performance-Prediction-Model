# Attributes for student.csv (Math and Portuguese lang. course) datasets:

1) sex - student's sex (binary: "F" - female or "M" - male)
2) age - student's age (numeric: from 15 to 22)
3) Medu - mother's education (numeric: 0 - none,  1 - primary education (4th grade), 2 – 5th to 9th grade, 3 – secondary education or 4 – higher education)
4) Fedu - father's education (numeric: 0 - none,  1 - primary education (4th grade), 2 – 5th to 9th grade, 3 – secondary education or 4 – higher education)
5) traveltime - home to school travel time (numeric: 1 - <15 min., 2 - 15 to 30 min., 3 - 30 min. to 1 hour, or 4 - >1 hour)
6) studytime - weekly study time (numeric: 1 - <2 hours, 2 - 2 to 5 hours, 3 - 5 to 10 hours, or 4 - >10 hours)
7) failures - number of past class failures (numeric: n if 1<=n<3, else 4)
8) schoolsup - extra educational support (binary: yes or no)
9) famsup - family educational support (binary: yes or no)
10) paid - extra paid classes within the course subject (Math or Portuguese) (binary: yes or no)
11) activities - extra-curricular activities (binary: yes or no)
12) higher - wants to take higher education (binary: yes or no)
13) internet - Internet access at home (binary: yes or no)
14) romantic - with a romantic relationship (binary: yes or no)
15) famrel - quality of family relationships (numeric: from 1 - very bad to 5 - excellent)
16) freetime - free time after school (numeric: from 1 - very low to 5 - very high)
17) goout - going out with friends (numeric: from 1 - very low to 5 - very high)
18) health - current health status (numeric: from 1 - very bad to 5 - very good)
19) absences - number of school absences (numeric: from 0 to 93)

# these are grades:
20) G1 - first period grade (numeric: from 0 to 20)
21) G2 - second period grade (numeric: from 0 to 20)
22) G3 - final grade (numeric: from 0 to 20, output target)
